How to Play

  • Move: Use the arrow keys to move left, right, or down.
  • Rotate: Press the up arrow key to rotate the piece.
  • Drop: Press Space to instantly drop a piece.
  • Clear Rows: Fill a row completely to remove it and earn points.
  • Game Over: The game ends when pieces stack to the top.
  • Start/Pause: Press the start button to begin. Press Esc to pause.
